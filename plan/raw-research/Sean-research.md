# Types of Players

1. Clarie:

    Clarie represents a player motivated by flow-state escapism rather than financial gain. The psychological framework for this persona is heavily grounded in modern anthropology and behavioral conditioning.

    [Sensory Escapism](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC8866259/)

2. Ben: 
    
    Ben represents the analytical player who tries to apply skill, strategy, and efficiency to a game of pure chance. 

    [Examining the illusion of control via outcome sequences in online slot machine gambling](https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/full/10.1080/16066359.2025.2581087#:~:text=In%20its%20original%20formulation%2C%20there,%2C%20an%20element%20of%20competition%2C)

3. Bob:

    Bob represents the modern target audience for Responsible Gambling (RG) design. He wants to play but is wary of the predatory mechanics that distort a player's sense of time and money.

    [Losses disguised as wins in multiline slots](https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/full/10.1080/14459795.2017.1355404#abstract)
