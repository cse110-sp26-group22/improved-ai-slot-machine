# AI Slot Machine Research Overview:

## Principles that make games fun/engaging: 

- Having a consistent, unified style 
	- Creating a cohesive atmosphere and mood that ties the game together
	- Optionally having some story/context/lore which grounds the world of the game
- Having feedback loops where players' actions are meaningful
	- Tie player victories to gameplay progression, keeping them engaged
- Give players choices to make
	- Implement strategic choices to make players' decisions meaningful
	- Implement customization choices to make players attached to the game

## Principles that make chance-based games addicting:

- Variable reinforcement
	- Intermittent rewards can entice users to repeatedly player
- Knowledge of the possibility of a large reward
	- Emphasizing the amount of reward over the probability of obtaining that reward
	- Individuals gamble more when the apparent possibility of obtaining a reward is larger
	- Individuals gamble more when they feel as if their actions have a significant influence over the final outcome

## How slot machines work:

- Slot machines have "reels" that have a number of symbols. Digital technology enhances slot machines where they could possibly have symbols 
of 256 or more.

- Payout depends on the machine.

- Online slots have RNG, which is an algorithm that ensures random output of the machine. 

- RTP (Return to player) is percentage of total wagers paid out to players in winnings. Statistical average calculated over billions of spins. 

- Average RTP of online slots around 96%.

- Volatility defines frequency of winning spins slot will produce. 
	- Likely to land more winning spins if playing on slots with low volatility.

## Visuals for user experience: 

- High quality visuals create atmosphere.

- Graphics establish emotional setting of the game. 

- Players are drawn to visually stimulating content.

- Games that use vibrant colors, smooth transitions, and thematic consistency tend to create excitement and anticipation.

- Immersion keeps players engaged for longer periods of time

- Technological advancements have improved the quality of online slot machines	
	- 3D animations, interactive bonuses, real-time lighting, physics-based graphics
